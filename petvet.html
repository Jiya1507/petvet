Blood Donation

Pet blood donation is a vital process that helps save the lives of dogs in need. Here are the key details: Eligibility: Donors must be healthy, aged 1-8 years, weigh at least 50 pounds, and be current on vaccinations. They should have a calm temperament and no history of previous transfusions24. Donation Process: The procedure takes about 5-10 minutes. Dogs are prepped with a health check, and blood is collected from the jugular vein using sterile equipment. Afterward, they receive treats and hydration35. Blood Processing: Collected blood is processed to separate components (red cells, plasma) and can help multiple dogs. Red cells last about six weeks, while plasma can be frozen for up to five years 12. Post-Donation Care: Dogs should rest for the day following donation, but they can resume normal activities the next day
